About PhilLaw - PH Legal AI Assistant

Welcome to PhilLaw AI, your dedicated Artificial Intelligence assistant focused exclusively on Philippine legal matters. We're built on the Gemini model and tailored to help citizens, students, and legal professionals quickly access and understand complex Philippine laws.

Our Mission

Our mission is to democratize legal information in the Philippines. We strive to provide clear, structured, and accessible summaries of Philippine statutes, legal doctrines, and procedures. We believe that informed citizens are empowered citizens.

What We Do

  • Provide Structured Legal Analysis: Every answer is organized into clear sections: [Legal Issue], [Applicable Laws], [Analysis], and [Recommendations].
  • Focus Exclusively on PH Law: We handle questions related to the Revised Penal Code, Civil Code, Special Laws (like RA 9262 and RA 8485), and more.
  • Maintain Language Context: We respond in the same language you use ( English, Bisaya, Filipino etc.) for better comprehension.

Important Disclaimer

Please remember that PhilLaw is an AI information tool and not a substitute for a licensed lawyer. The responses provided are based on available legal texts and are for general informational and educational purposes only. For personalized legal advice, case-specific strategy, or representation, always consult a qualified Philippine attorney.
